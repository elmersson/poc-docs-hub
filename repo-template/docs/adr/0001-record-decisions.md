# ADR 0001: Record architecture decisions

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Decisions and their trade-offs get lost in chat threads and heads.

## Decision
We record architecturally significant decisions as ADRs in docs/adr/, one decision per file, append-only.

## Consequences
Newcomers and AI agents can read why things are the way they are. Superseded decisions stay visible.
